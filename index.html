About Gava Tana

Gava Tana is a social listening and campaign intelligence platform designed for the Tana River County Senatorial Campaign 2027. We analyze publicly available Facebook data to understand community concerns, track sentiment, and identify key issues affecting residents across all sub-counties including Hola, Galole, Bura, Garsen, Tana Delta, and Wayu.

Our platform is read-only. We do not post, comment, or interact with any content on Facebook. All data processed is publicly available information.
