Data Deletion Request

In compliance with Kenya Data Protection Act 2019

Your Rights Under Kenyan Law

The Kenya Data Protection Act 2019 gives you the right to request deletion of your personal data from our systems. If you believe we have collected your information from public Facebook content and you wish to have it removed, please follow one of the methods below.

How to Request Data Deletion

Method 1: Email Request (Recommended)

Send an email to the address below with the subject line "Data Deletion Request". Please include:

  • Your full name as it appears on Facebook
  • Your Facebook profile URL or User ID (if known)
  • A brief statement that you request deletion of your data

Method 2: Web Form (Coming Soon)

🔗 https://demo-one-zeta-92.vercel.app/data-deletion-callback

What happens after you request deletion?
  • We will verify your identity using the information you provide
  • We will locate any data associated with your Facebook profile
  • We will permanently delete your data from our systems
  • You will receive a confirmation email within 30 days
  • Your data will be removed from all backups within 90 days

What Data We Collect and Delete

When you request deletion, we will remove:

  • Your public Facebook display name and user ID
  • Any public comments you made that we collected
  • Any public posts you made that we collected
  • Your public location information
  • Any analysis or sentiment tags associated with your content

Note: We cannot delete content that originated on Facebook. That content remains on Facebook's servers subject to their policies. We only delete our copy of publicly available data.

Data That Cannot Be Deleted Immediately

In some cases, data may be retained for legal or operational reasons:

  • Aggregated statistics that no longer identify individuals
  • Data required for legal compliance or dispute resolution
  • Backup copies (which will be deleted within 90 days)

Confirmation Timeline

We process all deletion requests within 30 days as required by Kenyan law. You will receive:

  1. Acknowledgement Email: Within 48 hours confirming we received your request
  2. Completion Email: Within 30 days confirming your data has been deleted
