Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 3, 2026 | Last Updated: May 3, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Gava Tana ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our campaign intelligence platform. We are committed to protecting your privacy and complying with the Kenya Data Protection Act 2019.

1. Information We Collect

Gava Tana is a political campaign intelligence platform that collects publicly available information from Facebook Pages and Groups. This includes:

  • Public Posts & Comments: Text content of publicly visible posts and comments
  • Public Profile Information: Display names and public user IDs of commenters
  • Public Location Data: Location information users have chosen to make public on their profiles or in posts
  • Public Reaction Data: Counts of likes, shares, and reactions (not individual identities)
  • Public Page/Group Information: Names and metadata of public pages and groups we monitor
Important: We only collect data that is already publicly visible on Facebook. We do NOT collect private messages, private profile information, or any data from private groups.

2. How We Use Information

We use the collected information solely for legitimate campaign research purposes:

  • Sentiment Analysis: Understanding public opinion about governance issues in Tana River County
  • Issue Tracking: Identifying community concerns about roads, water, security, education, health, and other topics
  • Campaign Intelligence: Helping the Tana River Senatorial Campaign understand voter priorities
  • Super-Supporter Identification: Finding influential community members who actively engage positively with campaign content
  • Strategic Planning: Data-driven decision making for campaign resource allocation

3. Legal Basis for Processing (Kenya Data Protection Act 2019)

Our processing is based on:

  • Legitimate Interests: Political research and campaign intelligence is a legitimate interest for democratic processes
  • Publicly Available Information: All data processed is already in the public domain
  • Consent: By making your Facebook content public, you have consented to it being viewed by the public

4. Data Storage and Security

  • Location: Data is stored on secure servers with encryption at rest and in transit
  • Retention: We retain data for the duration of the campaign period (through 2027), after which it is anonymized or deleted
  • Access Control: Only authorized campaign personnel have access to the platform
  • Security Measures: We implement industry-standard security measures including encryption, firewalls, and regular security audits

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do NOT sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We only share data in the following limited circumstances:

  • Internal Campaign Use: Data is used exclusively for the Tana River Senatorial Campaign
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or court order
  • Service Providers: With third-party vendors who help us operate the platform (subject to confidentiality agreements)

6. Your Rights

Under the Kenya Data Protection Act 2019, you have the following rights:

  • Right to Access: Request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Right to Rectification: Correct inaccurate information
  • Right to Deletion: Request deletion of your data (see Data Deletion page)
  • Right to Object: Object to our processing of your data
  • Right to Restrict Processing: Limit how we use your data

7. Data Deletion Requests

To request deletion of your data from our platform:

  1. Visit our Data Deletion Request page
  2. Send an email to nekshenjackson@gmail.com with subject "Data Deletion Request"
  3. Include your Facebook name and, if possible, your Facebook User ID
  4. We will process your request within 30 days

8. Children's Privacy

Our platform is not intended for individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

9. International Data Transfers

Data may be stored on servers located outside Kenya. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for any international data transfers.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
